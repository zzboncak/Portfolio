About Me

Hi, I’m Zack Zboncak (zuh-bon-sack).

I am a web developer and graduate of Thinkful’s Engineering Nights and Weekends program. I’ve always had a love for technology and solving problems, and have recently discoverd my love for coding. Nothing is more satisfying than taking an idea in your head, implementing it in code, and watching it work succesfully. While you’ll primarily find Apple products in my home, I love gadgets of any kind, toying with them, and using them to make life easier.

My expertise is in the Javascript tech stack (Vanilla JS, React.js, Node.js). I also have a strong understanding of HTML, CSS, and PostgreSQL. As a life-long learner, I strive to continually add to my skill set (currently learning Python and Ruby). I love building whatever projects pop into my head, and am currently looking to work on a team of developers building exciting software.

When I’m not coding, I’m generally spending time with my wife and 3 very energetic young boys, from whom I’m often inspired to create new projects (see my basic math practice app). We love camping, nature walks, and family movie nights. As a person, I love a good book, a good craft beer, and spending time with the people who mean the most to me.

Fun fact: If I really like a movie or book, I tend to buy multiple copies (for reasons unbeknownst to me or my wife).

Brewery Shuffle

About: Simply select a state and city and hit "SHUFFLE!" and see a randomized list of breweries in your area. Click on the brewery's link for more info, or check out the map to see how close it is to you.
Who it's for: Brewery Shuffle is the answer to the beer enthusiast's constant problem: with so many great craft breweries out there, how do I pick which one to try?
Why I built it: The craft beer industry has exploded over the last several years. While there are some easy go-to spots in most major areas, there are some hidden gems you might not be aware of. Brewery shuffle helps you try new breweries around you.
