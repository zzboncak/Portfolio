About Me

Hi, I’m Zack Zboncak (zuh-bon-sack), and yes, my full name is Zachary, but I spell my name with a "k" when shortened.

I am a web developer and soon-to-be graduate of Thinkful’s Engineering Nights and Weekends program. I’ve always had a love for technology and solving problems, and have recently discoverd my love for coding. Nothing is more satisfying than taking an idea in your head, implementing it in code, and watching it work succesfully. While you’ll primarily find Apple products in my home, I love gadgets of any kind, toying with them, and using them to make life easier.

When I’m not coding, I’m generally spending time with my wife and 3 very energetic young boys, from whom I’m often inspired to create new projects (see my basic math practice app). We love camping, nature walks, and family movie nights. As a person, I love a good book, a good craft beer, and spending time with the people who mean the most to me.

Fun fact: If I really like a movie or book, I tend to buy multiple copies (for reasons unbeknownst to me or my wife).
